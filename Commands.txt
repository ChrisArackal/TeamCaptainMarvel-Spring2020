|___________________________________________________________
|User input       |Action                                  |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"North" or "N"   |Player Moves to North Room if available |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"South" or "S"   |Player Moves to South Room if available |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"East" or "E"    |Player Moves to East Room if available  |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"West" or "W"    |Player Moves to West  Room if available |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"Exit"           |Player exits game                       |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"Inspect"        |Displays Room's inspect message         |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"Check Inventory"|Displays Players's item inventory       |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"Search Room"    |Displays Room's inventory               |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"Pickup" +       |Adds item from room's inventory to the  |
|"Item Name"      |player's inventory.                     |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"Drop" +         |Drops item form player inventory to the |
|"Item Name"      |room's inventory.                       |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"Inspect" +      |Displays the item's description.        |
|"Item Name"      |                                        |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"Use" +          |Equips Item and Player receives attack  |
|"Item Name"      | bonus if available during fight        |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"Consume" +      |Increases players health points if item |
|"Item Name"      |has health bonus. (if in inventory)     |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"Save Game"      |Saves Current Game State                |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"Attack"         |Attacks monster.Calculates damage.Prints|
|                 | (while in fight)                       | 
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"runaway"        |Exits in the middle of a fight          | 
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"attempt puzzle" |takes game to puzzle mode               |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"Solve Puzzle"   |Starts Puzzle and asks for answers      |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"help"           |displays hint for puzzle if unlocked    |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
|"giveup"         |quits puzzle and returns to current room|
|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
